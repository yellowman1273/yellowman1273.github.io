Planet Texture Maps: Earth

This page provides all seasons of the year on Earth. These maps were taken off of NASA's Visible Earth thing (credits to NASA Earth Observatory). According to https://earthobservatory.nasa.gov/features/BlueMarble, you should credit the NASA Earth Observatory when using these maps.
